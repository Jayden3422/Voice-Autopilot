# Jayden Platform Overview

## What is Jayden?

Jayden is an AI-powered voice assistant and support automation platform designed for businesses of all sizes. Our platform combines cutting-edge natural language processing, real-time voice synthesis, and intelligent workflow automation to help companies deliver exceptional customer experiences while dramatically reducing operational costs.

Founded in 2023, Jayden serves over 2,500 businesses across 40 countries, handling more than 12 million customer interactions per month.

## Core Platform Features

### Intelligent Voice Assistant
- **Natural Conversation**: Our voice assistant understands context, handles interruptions, and maintains multi-turn conversations just like a human agent.
- **Multilingual Support**: Supports 28 languages out of the box, including English, Mandarin Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, and Arabic.
- **Voice Cloning**: Create a custom brand voice or choose from 50+ professionally recorded voice profiles.
- **Emotion Detection**: Real-time sentiment analysis adjusts tone and escalation paths based on caller mood.

### Support Automation Engine
- **Ticket Routing**: AI-powered ticket classification and routing with 96.3% accuracy, ensuring customers reach the right team on the first try.
- **Knowledge Base RAG**: Retrieval-augmented generation pulls answers directly from your documentation, product manuals, and FAQ databases.
- **Auto-Resolution**: Handles common inquiries end-to-end without human intervention, including password resets, order tracking, appointment scheduling, and billing questions.
- **Agent Copilot**: When human agents are needed, Jayden provides real-time suggestions, relevant knowledge articles, and drafted responses.

### Analytics and Insights
- **Real-Time Dashboard**: Monitor call volume, resolution rates, average handle time, and customer satisfaction scores in real time.
- **Conversation Intelligence**: Automatic call summarization, topic extraction, and trend analysis across all interactions.
- **Custom Reports**: Build and schedule custom reports with drag-and-drop report builder. Export to CSV, PDF, or integrate with your BI tools.
- **Quality Assurance**: Automated QA scoring evaluates 100% of interactions against your quality criteria.

### Integration Ecosystem
Jayden connects seamlessly with the tools your team already uses:
- **CRM**: Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive
- **Helpdesk**: Zendesk, Freshdesk, Intercom, ServiceNow
- **Communication**: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Twilio, RingCentral
- **Calendar**: Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, Calendly
- **E-commerce**: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento
- **Custom**: RESTful API and webhook support for any system
