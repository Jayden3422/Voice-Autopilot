# Jayden Pricing Plans

## Overview

Jayden offers three pricing tiers designed to scale with your business. All plans include a **14-day free trial** with full access to features -- no credit card required.

Pricing is based on the number of AI-handled interactions per month. An "interaction" is defined as a single customer conversation, whether by voice call, chat, or messaging channel.

---

## Starter Plan -- $99/month

**Best for**: Small businesses and startups handling up to 500 interactions per month.

### Included Features
- Up to **500 AI interactions/month**
- 1 voice assistant persona
- 5 supported languages
- Basic analytics dashboard
- Email support (response within 24 hours)
- Knowledge base RAG with up to 100 documents
- Pre-built integrations: Slack, Google Calendar, Zendesk
- Standard voice library (15 voice profiles)
- Conversation history retained for 30 days

### Overage
- Additional interactions billed at **$0.25 each**
- Overage cap can be set to prevent surprise charges

---

## Pro Plan -- $499/month

**Best for**: Growing businesses handling up to 5,000 interactions per month who need advanced features and customization.

### Included Features
- Up to **5,000 AI interactions/month**
- 5 voice assistant personas
- 15 supported languages
- Advanced analytics with custom reports
- Priority email and chat support (response within 4 hours)
- Knowledge base RAG with up to 1,000 documents
- All integrations (CRM, helpdesk, e-commerce, calendar)
- Full voice library (50+ profiles) + 1 custom voice clone
- Emotion detection and sentiment analysis
- Agent Copilot for human agents
- Automated QA scoring
- Conversation history retained for 12 months
- Webhook and API access (10,000 API calls/month)

### Overage
- Additional interactions billed at **$0.15 each**
- Additional API calls billed at $0.005 each

## Billing and Payment

- **Billing Cycle**: Monthly or annual. Annual plans receive a **15% discount**.
- **Payment Methods**: Credit card, ACH bank transfer, wire transfer (Enterprise only).
- **Currency**: Billed in CAD. Invoices available in EUR, GBP, and CNY upon request.
- **Cancellation**: Cancel anytime. No long-term contracts on Starter and Pro plans.
- **Refund Policy**: Pro-rated refund available within the first 30 days.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Pricing

**Can I switch plans at any time?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are pro-rated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Is there a free tier?**
We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. We do not currently offer a permanent free tier, but our Starter plan is affordable for most small businesses.

**Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?**
Yes. Qualified nonprofits and educational institutions receive a 25% discount on any plan. Contact support@Jayden.com with proof of eligibility.
